Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 03, 2024

Thank you for choosing Lift Logr. This Privacy Policy outlines how Lift Logr (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use our mobile application (“the App”).

Information We Collect

Lift Logr does not collect any personal information from its users. The App operates on a local-only basis, meaning all data input and generated by the user is stored locally on the user's device and never leave the device.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information in any way. Lift Logr solely operates to assist users in tracking their lifting routines and does not share, sell, or otherwise distribute any information gathered by the App.

Data Security

All data input and generated by the user is stored locally on the user's device; using Apple’s data base framework, Swift Data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be reflected in the updated version of the App, along with the effective date. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
